About Our Agency

Tai Connection Football Agency was founded in 2010 with a mission to provide world-class representation for football's most promising talents. Our team of experienced agents has over 5 years of combined experience in the football industry.

We pride ourselves on our personalized approach, ensuring each player receives the attention and guidance they need to maximize their potential both on and off the pitch.

Our network spans across Africa's top leagues, with strong relationships at Rwanda premier league, Tusker premier league,Uganda Premier League, Bundesliga, and Ligue 1 clubs.
